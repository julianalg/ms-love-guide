Tip 1: Memorize your roots and lexicon words!

Create flashcards or a Quizlet for each of the greek/latin roots and lexicon words every week, and review them in your downtime to ensure you have them memorized!

Tip 2: Have a good knowledge of history!

By having a basic understanding of history, including Victorian Europe and World War II, you can have a deeper understanding of the nuance of certain books.
