Welcome to Ms. Love's Classroom!

This is a guide to everything you need to know for Ms. Love's class and 10th grade in general!

English Tips Sophomore Tips

  • Tip 1: Stay organized!

    Keep a calendar or to-do list with all of your assignments and due dates. This helps you stay organized and on-top of every assignment.

  • Tip 2: Care for your mental health

    Make sure you take breaks from studying, sleep and eat enough, and try not to push yourself too much. All of these and practing geenral self-care will help you prevent burnout and stay healthy.

  • Tip 3: Build a study group

    Create a group chat with friends where you can all study and ask questions if necessary. If it would help, try also going out to group study sessions.

  • Tip 4: Try to use all resources possible

    If you're stuck on a specific concept or topic, try to consult as many resources that are available to you, including teachers, peers, books or the internet. This allows you to keep moving even if you are having trouble grasping a certain idea or concept.

  • Tip 5: Join new clubs!

    10th grade is a great time to try to branch out into new activities. Try to reach out of your comfort zone to meet new people and get new experiences

  • Tip 6: Develop good study habits

    Especially if this is the first time you're taking AP classes or exams, developing good study habits like active recall or writing effective notes lets you review and retain more information and perform better on exams.

  • Tip 7: Prevent distractions

    Try to find a quiet area with little distractions to do your homework, and try to lockdown your phone or computer to prevent distractions. This helps you become more focused on your work and makes you more productive to finish your assignments.

  • Tip 8: Set goals for yourself

    Set both short-term and long-term for your academic, personal and extracurriculars. These help give you direction and motivation in your work.

  • Tip 9: Build positive relationships

    Try to surround yourself with supportive and positive friends that uplift you and motivate you to do your best.

  • Tip 10: Develop time management skills

    Try to identify which tasks are the most important and tackle those first, and break larger projects into smaller more achievable tasks. Learning how to effectively manage your time allows you to achieve more and focus on what is really important.
